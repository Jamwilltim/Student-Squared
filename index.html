Get in touch and let us equip your child with the confidence, knowledge and mindset to succeed. We will only ask you to pay if you are 100% satisfied with our service.

Style of Teaching

Although the style of teaching provided is tailored to the individual, Student Squared have overarching techniques that we implement to maximise development. For example, our tutors do not do student’s homework with them. We advise that homework should be done independently, as it is set by a teacher who has matched the task with their current stage of learning. Instead, we prefer to go through marked homework which allows tutors to identify weaknesses and then explain the correct ways to achieve maximum possible marks. People learn more from failure than from success. We also have a strong focus on past exam papers as it builds exam confidence, identifies areas of weakness and by reviewing mark schemes, students can familiarise themselves with what examiners want to see. Ultimately though, our teaching style is devised on a case-by-case basis to maximise individual potential.

Getting Started With Us

At Student Squared, we understand that every student is unique. That’s why we offer a free consultation meeting with one of our representatives to match your child with the most suitable tutor (exam board, learning style, current proficiency, special accommodations etc.). We then offer another free consultation meeting with the selected tutor to discuss educational goals and objectives, along with any other requests. Once tutoring begins, we strive to have open lines of communication from tutor to parent with regular updates on student progression. There are also clear and responsive methods to reach a company representative to ensure any requests/issues can be dealt with as quickly as possible. If you are not 100% satisfied with the service you receive, we will not ask you to pay.

Student Tutors?

We understand that the tutor you choose will significantly alter your child’s future. When chosen correctly, there are clear benefits to a student tutor over the traditional alternatives:
  • Relatability – Student tutors are closer in age to the students they teach. When a student feels like they can relate to a tutor and vice versa, it promotes an open and honest relationship. This creates a positive learning environment. It also allows our tutors to have a greater understanding of the student, helping cater for individual needs and preferences, enabling students reach their full potential.
  • Current Understanding – Our tutors have a current understanding of the material and the way it’s being taught in the classroom. They also remember how it felt to not know the subject, and the steps they took to learn and then go on to excel in their subject. This makes it easier for them to explain the content in a way that is understandable and relevant to the student.
  • Exam preparation – Your child’s tutor will have taken the exams they’re teaching towards. They can share subject knowledge with students, but more importantly, methods and strategies to convert that knowledge into the maximum marks achievable.
  • Mentorship – We teach tutors how to act as mentors through providing guidance, support and role modelling for academic, social and personal development. They will help your child set and achieve goals, build self-esteem and further develop skills such as problem solving and communication – allowing them to navigate the challenges of school and develop into adulthood.
  • Cost effective – Our tutors charge lower rates than the market average, making it a more affordable option for families. It also means that a student could have more sessions than their peers using traditional methods - for the same price.
